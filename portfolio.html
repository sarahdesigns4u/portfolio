Transportation Station: Charting Our Classroom Routes

The transportation chart is a visual aid prominently displayed in the classroom to help organize and track students' daily transportation arrangements. It serves as a valuable reference for both the teacher and students, ensuring that everyone is aware of each student's transportation plans and facilitating a smooth dismissal process at the end of the day.

Tools Used: PowerPoint, Canva
